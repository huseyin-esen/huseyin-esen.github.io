I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Chemistry at Kocaeli University, where I have been working since 2020. I have over 25 years of experience in polymer and organic chemistry, with a particular focus on photoinitiated polymerization, UV-curable coatings, polymer nanocomposites, and functional polymer synthesis.

In addition to my academic role, I have been working as an independent consultant for companies in the chemical and polymer industries since 2016, helping clients optimize processes, develop new products, and solve complex technical challenges.

## Research Interests

- Photoinitiated polymerization and UV-curable systems
- Polymer nanocomposites (clay/polymer, hybrid materials)
- Functional and dendritic polymer synthesis
- Antibacterial polymer coatings and thermosets
- Biodegradable and specialty materials

## Selected Publications

- Photoinduced insolubilization of SBS-acrylate films of high thickness under 405 nm
- Synthesis and characterization of linear dendritic homo and copolymers acrylated behera amine
- Polymers: UV-Cured Polymer Nanocomposites
- Light induced curing of Clay/polymer nanocomposites
- Simultaneous photoinduced electron transfer and photoinduced CuAAC processes for antibacterial thermosets

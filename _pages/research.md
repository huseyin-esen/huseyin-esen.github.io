## Research Interests

My research focuses on polymer chemistry and photochemistry, with emphasis on functional materials and sustainable applications.

### Photoinitiated Polymerization & UV-Curable Systems
Development of photoinitiator systems and UV-curable coatings for industrial applications. Investigation of photoinduced electron transfer and radical polymerization mechanisms under visible and UV light.

### Polymer Nanocomposites
Synthesis and characterization of clay/polymer and hybrid nanocomposites using light-induced crosslinking polymerization. Applications in barrier coatings and advanced materials.

### Functional & Dendritic Polymer Synthesis
Design and synthesis of linear-dendritic block copolymers and acrylated macromolecules for specialized material properties.

### Antibacterial Polymer Coatings
Development of thermosets and coatings with antibacterial activity via simultaneous photoinduced processes (CuAAC, electron transfer).

### Bio-based & Biodegradable Materials
Synthesis of monomers and polymers from plant oils (castor oil, epoxidized triglycerides) for sustainable applications. Development of biodegradable protective materials.
